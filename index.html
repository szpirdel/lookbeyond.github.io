The story behind it

Why I'm building this

The most valuable insights often live at the intersections between existing documents, papers, and articles — not in any single source. Today, that kind of discovery is reserved for well-funded institutions with large research teams. I want to change that.

LookBeyond is built on one simple conviction: knowledge discovery shouldn't depend on what you already know to look for. Whether you're tracking geopolitical events, researching across disciplines, or investigating complex networks of actors — the connections are already there. You just need the right tool to see them.
